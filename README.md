## ğŸŒ Understanding Air Quality Data
Air quality data includes various metrics such as PM2.5, PM10, and AQI (Air Quality Index). Here is a brief overview of these terms:

- **PM2.5**: Particulate matter smaller than 2.5 micrometers. This can affect health if levels are high.
- **PM10**: Particulate matter smaller than 10 micrometers. Not as harmful as PM2.5 but still important to monitor.
- **AQI**: A measure used to communicate how polluted the air currently is.
